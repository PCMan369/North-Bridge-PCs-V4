The Process

How a Custom Build Works

There's no complicated form to fill out. Just reach out, tell me what you're working with, and we'll go from there. Most of this happens over a few emails.

I won't spec out a build that's way over your budget, and I won't pad the parts list to inflate the price. The goal is a machine that fits what you actually need.

Start the Conversation →

No Tech Knowledge Required

Not Sure What Hardware You Need?

That's fine — most people aren't. You don't need to know what GPU you want or what CPU tier makes sense. That's the part I figure out.

What actually helps: knowing your budget, knowing what games you play, and knowing whether there's anything specific you care about — like being able to add a monitor later, or wanting the system to be quiet, or wanting Linux.

If you don't know the answers to any of those either, just say so. We can work backwards from "I want to play X and I have around $Y to spend" and I'll figure out a reasonable parts list from there.
